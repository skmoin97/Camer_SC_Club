Welcome to Camer SC

Welcome to Camer SC Inc. Located in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), Camer SC is a social, non-profit, non-religious, and non-political association that aims to manage and operate a soccer club within the Greater Toronto Area. The club is fostered on providing its members the opportunity to play soccer through an organized, team-oriented program and to compete at levels consistent with individual interests and abilities.
